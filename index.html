About Me

I am currently a third year MSc. student in School of Remote Sensing and Information Engineering, Wuhan University (WHU), majoring Geographic Information System. I am a student member of the Lab. of Intelligent Information Processing (IIP) supervised by Prof. Zhenzhong Chen. Before that, I received the B. Eng. degree in Remote Sensing Science and Technology from Wuhan University in 2016. My curriculum vitae is here: CV.

My research interest includes data mining and machine learning. And I have research experience on enhancing recommender systems with heterogeneous side information, multimedia data mining such as micro-video popularity predition. In addition, I am experienced in projects on data mining, including user profile analysis in social newtworks, data analysis in LBSNs.

Currently, I focus on the multimedia data mining.
